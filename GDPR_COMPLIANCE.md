# GDPR Compliance Documentation

## Overview

AgentForge is committed to compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for our European users.

## Legal Basis for Processing

### 1. Contract (Art. 6(1)(b))
- Account creation and management
- Service delivery
- Payment processing
- Workflow execution

### 2. Consent (Art. 6(1)(a))
- Marketing communications
- Optional analytics
- Cookie usage (non-essential)

### 3. Legitimate Interest (Art. 6(1)(f))
- Security monitoring
- Service improvement
- Fraud prevention
- Analytics (anonymized)

## Data Subject Rights

### Right to Access (Art. 15)
**Implementation**: 
- In-app: Settings > Privacy > Download My Data
- Email request: privacy@agentforge.io
- Response time: Within 30 days
- Format: JSON export

### Right to Rectification (Art. 16)
**Implementation**:
- Edit profile in app
- Contact support for corrections
- Immediate effect

### Right to Erasure (Art. 17)
**Implementation**:
- In-app: Settings > Account > Delete Account
- Confirmation required
- 30-day retention for backups
- Exceptions: Legal obligations, financial records

### Right to Data Portability (Art. 20)
**Implementation**:
- Export workflows as JSON
- Export account data
- Machine-readable format
- Transfer to another service

### Right to Object (Art. 21)
**Implementation**:
- Opt-out of marketing emails
- Disable optional analytics
- In-app privacy controls

### Right to Restriction (Art. 18)
**Implementation**:
- Temporarily suspend processing
- Email: privacy@agentforge.io

## Data Protection Measures

### Technical Measures
- Encryption at rest (AES-256)
- Encryption in transit (TLS 1.3)
- Access controls and authentication
- Regular security audits
- Pseudonymization where possible
- Data minimization

### Organizational Measures
- Privacy by Design and Default
- Staff training on data protection
- Data Protection Impact Assessments (DPIA)
- Data breach notification procedures
- Third-party processor agreements

## Data Processing Records

### Categories of Data Processed
1. **Identification Data**: Name, email
2. **Account Data**: Password (hashed), preferences
3. **Usage Data**: Workflows, executions
4. **Payment Data**: Processed by Stripe (not stored)
5. **Technical Data**: IP address, device info, logs

### Purposes of Processing
1. Service delivery
2. Payment processing
3. Customer support
4. Security and fraud prevention
5. Service improvement

### Data Recipients
1. Cloud hosting providers (AWS)
2. Payment processor (Stripe)
3. Email service (SendGrid)
4. Analytics (Mixpanel - anonymized)
5. Error tracking (Sentry)

### International Transfers
- **Mechanism**: Standard Contractual Clauses
- **Countries**: USA (AWS, Stripe)
- **Safeguards**: Encryption, contractual protections

## Data Breach Response

### Detection
- Automated monitoring
- Security alerts
- Regular audits

### Response Procedure
1. **0-24 hours**: Contain and assess breach
2. **24-72 hours**: Notify supervisory authority if required
3. **Within 72 hours**: Notify affected users if high risk
4. **Ongoing**: Remediation and prevention

### Notification Template
- Nature of breach
- Data categories affected
- Approximate number of users
- Likely consequences
- Measures taken
- Contact point for information

- Process only on our instructions

## Cookie Policy

### Cookie Categories

#### Strictly Necessary
- Session management
- Authentication tokens
- Security features
- **Consent**: Not required (essential)

#### Functional
- User preferences
- Language selection
- **Consent**: Implied consent

#### Analytics
- Usage statistics
- Performance monitoring
- **Consent**: Explicit consent required

#### Marketing
- Personalized content
- Email campaigns
- **Consent**: Explicit consent required

- Manage in Settings > Privacy > Cookies
- Browser settings
- Cookie banner on first use

## Children's Data

We do not knowingly process data of children under 13 (or 16 in EU).

**If we discover underage users**:
1. Immediately suspend account
2. Delete all personal data
3. Notify parent/guardian

## Data Retention Schedule

| Data Type | Retention Period | Legal Basis |
|-----------|-----------------|-------------|
| Account data | Until deletion + 30 days | Contract |
| Workflows | Until deletion + 30 days | Contract |
| Execution logs | 90 days | Legitimate interest |
| Payment records | 7 years | Legal obligation |
| Support tickets | 3 years | Legitimate interest |
| Marketing data | Until consent withdrawn | Consent |
| Backups | 30 days | Legitimate interest |

## User Rights Request Process

### How to Request
1. **Email**: privacy@agentforge.io
2. **In-app**: Settings > Privacy > Data Rights
3. **Subject line**: "GDPR Request - [Your Right]"

### Information Required
- Full name
- Account email
- Specific right being exercised
- Identity verification

### Response Timeline
- **Acknowledgment**: Within 3 days
- **Response**: Within 30 days (extendable to 60 for complex requests)
- **Free of charge**: First request free, reasonable fee for repeated requests
