# Privacy Policy for AgentForge

**Last Updated: February 10, 2026**

## Introduction

AgentForge ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Account Information**: Email address, name, password (encrypted)
- **Payment Information**: Processed by Stripe (we don't store card details)
- **Profile Information**: Avatar, preferences, settings

### Usage Information
- **Workflow Data**: Workflows, nodes, connections you create
- **Execution Logs**: Workflow execution history and results
- **API Keys**: Your AI provider API keys (encrypted on device)
- **Usage Metrics**: Feature usage, performance data
- **Device Information**: Device type, OS version, app version

### Automatically Collected
- **Log Data**: IP address, access times, pages viewed
- **Analytics Data**: App crashes, performance metrics
- **Cookies**: Session management, preferences

## How We Use Your Information

### To Provide Services
- Create and manage your account
- Process your workflows and AI agent tasks
- Execute API calls to AI providers using your keys
- Track credit usage and billing
- Provide customer support

### To Improve Services
- Analyze usage patterns
- Fix bugs and errors
- Develop new features
- Optimize performance

### For Communication
- Send service updates
- Notify about account activity
- Respond to inquiries
- Send promotional emails (with consent)

### For Security
- Detect and prevent fraud
- Monitor for abuse
- Enforce terms of service
- Protect user accounts

## Data Storage and Security

### Encryption
- **In Transit**: All data encrypted with TLS 1.3
- **At Rest**: Sensitive data encrypted with AES-256
- **API Keys**: Encrypted on device, never transmitted unencrypted
- **Passwords**: Hashed with bcrypt (10 rounds)

### Storage Locations
- **Primary Database**: PostgreSQL (secure cloud hosting)
- **Cache**: Redis (temporary data only)
- **Backups**: Encrypted daily backups, 30-day retention
- **File Storage**: AWS S3 with encryption

### Access Controls
- Role-based access control (RBAC)
- Multi-factor authentication for admin
- Audit logs for sensitive operations
- Regular security audits

## Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal information. We may share data with:

### Service Providers
- **Stripe**: Payment processing
- **AWS**: Cloud hosting and storage
- **SendGrid**: Email delivery
- **Sentry**: Error tracking
- **Mixpanel**: Analytics (anonymized)

### Legal Requirements
- To comply with laws
- To respond to legal requests
- To protect our rights
- To prevent fraud

### With Your Consent
- When you explicitly authorize sharing
- For features that require third-party integration

## Your Rights

### Under GDPR (EU Users)
- **Access**: Request your data
- **Rectification**: Correct inaccurate data
- **Erasure**: Delete your account and data
- **Portability**: Export your data
- **Objection**: Object to processing
- **Restriction**: Limit processing

### Under CCPA (California Users)
- **Know**: What data we collect
- **Delete**: Request deletion
- **Opt-out**: Opt-out of sale (we don't sell data)
- **Non-discrimination**: Equal service regardless of privacy choices

### How to Exercise Rights

Email: privacy@agentforge.io

Or in-app: Settings > Privacy > Data Rights

## Data Retention

- **Account Data**: Until account deletion + 30 days
- **Workflow Data**: Until workflow deletion + 30 days
- **Execution Logs**: 90 days
- **Transaction Records**: 7 years (legal requirement)
- **Analytics Data**: 24 months (anonymized)
- **Backups**: 30 days

## Children's Privacy
